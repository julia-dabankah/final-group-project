We dedicated this website to the forgotton women of history.
The women whose stories who have been eclipsed, marginalised,
and overshadowed. The women who deserve an honourable mention for being
exceptionally briliant.


The criteria we used to select these women were clear:
we wanted to select women who have exhibited outstandning
achievements in their field, but forgotten from the history
books.

CAROLINE CRIADO-PEREZ - author of 'Invisible Women'

“Women are set up to be forgettable. Ignorable. Dispensable - from culture, from history, a from data. And so, women become invisible.”
